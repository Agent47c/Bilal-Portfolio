How I Work

A Structured Pipeline For Perfect Results

No guesswork. I execute your video project through four distinct, communicative milestones.

01

Script & Raw Files

You share the scripts, hooks, raw files, and audio assets to outline the project's foundation.

02

Draft Creation

I construct the entire video draft, incorporating all necessary edits, visual pacing, and basic assembly.

03

Feedback & Revisions

You review the drafts and provide feedback on elements to adjust. I execute the revisions to perfect the video.

04

Final Delivery & Project Files

I deliver the final video. If needed, I can provide the project file (excluding heavy raw media due to size restrictions).
